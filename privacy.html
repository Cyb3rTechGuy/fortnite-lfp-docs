Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 7, 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect and store the following data:

  • Discord User ID: To identify you across servers and manage your sessions.
  • Game Account ID: To verify your in-game stats and rank via public APIs.
  • Guild (Server) ID: To apply server-specific settings (like log channels).
  • Usage Data: Timestamps of commands used for rate-limiting and debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely for:

  • Facilitating matchmaking (LFG) based on your skill level.
  • Preventing abuse (spam, smurfing, ban evasion).
  • Maintaining the security and stability of the service.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to outside parties. Data is only shared with Discord and Game APIs as strictly necessary to function.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Data is retained as long as you use the service. You may request full deletion of your data by contacting the developer via our support Discord.
